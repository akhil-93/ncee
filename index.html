Welcome to NCEE

The NCEE - Training Nucleus in Structural Engineering - arose from the intention to disseminate quality knowledge in the area of ​​Structural Engineering.

The creators of the Nucleus, all masters in Structural Engineering, with experience in the execution of projects and teaching in undergraduate and postgraduate studies, have systematized contents related to this large area, from basic to advanced levels.
