Course Description

The course presents the concepts needed for the design / dimensioning of steel structures using the NCEE methodology, a practical approach through an example of a shed design. The course also presents design criteria for this typology of structure, as well as the dimensioning of its elements, considering the use of free computational tools to aid in structural analysis and design.

Category : METAL STRUCTURES

General Information

Content
  • 1. Dimensioning of elements subjected to traction, compression, simple and composite bending, shear and combined forces;
  • 2. Sizing of full-beams;
  • 3. Scaffolding of columns;
  • 4. Sizing of bolted and welded connections;
  • 5. Base plate and anchors;
  • 6. Example of a shed.
